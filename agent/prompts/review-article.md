Review the provided blog post article. **Input:** Path to a markdown file or $@ for inline content.

---

## Author's Voice Profile

The author's authentic style includes:
- **Personal & conversational** - First person ("I", "I've"), personal stories
- **Strategic emphasis** - **bold** for key concepts, _italics_ for subtle emphasis
- **Practical examples** - Code snippets, real scenarios, actionable takeaways
- **Honest vulnerability** - Shares struggles, mistakes, learning moments
- **Structured flow** - Headers, lists, blockquotes, code blocks
- **Conversational closings** - Summaries, reflections, reader engagement

---

## Review Output

### 📋 Article Summary
1-2 sentence summary of the article's message.

### ✅ Strengths
What's working well (authentic moments, great explanations, effective structure).

### 🎯 Topic & Story Fit
- Does the topic align with typical subjects (development, personal projects, tutorials)?
- Does the narrative flow naturally?
- Any disconnects between opening story and main content?

### 📝 Structure Suggestions
Specific recommendations for headers, flow, transitions, opening hook, closing impact.

### 💡 Emphasis Opportunities
Suggest where **bold** or _italics_ could strengthen the message.

### 🔍 Spelling & Grammar
List typos, grammatical issues, awkward phrasings (quote exact text).

### 🎨 Clarity Improvements
Sentences that could be clearer, terms needing explanation, places for examples.

### ⚠️ Authenticity Check
- Any moments that feel inauthentic or forced?
- Does the voice stay consistent?
- Where could personality shine through more?

### 📌 Suggested Improvements
Numbered list of specific suggestions that enhance without altering the core concept.

---

## Guidelines

- **NEVER make edits** - Only suggestions
- **Preserve the author's voice** - Don't make it generic
- **Be specific** - Quote exact text
- **Explain why** - Give reasoning
- **Respect the core message** - Never alter the main concept
- **Celebrate authenticity** - Highlight genuine voice moments
