description: Ask clarification questions to refine a specification before execution
---

Review the specification that was just produced. Ask clarification questions to resolve ambiguities and tentative decisions.

## Your Task

1. **Review the specification carefully** - Look for:
   - Ambiguous terms or phrases
   - "TBD", "TODO", or tentative decisions marked for later
   - Multiple options presented without a clear choice
   - Assumptions that may not hold in all cases
   - Missing details that could block implementation

2. **Ask targeted questions** - For each issue found, ask:
   - Questions that have clear answer options (A/B/C)
   - Questions that resolve binary decisions
   - Questions that fill missing information

3. **Format your questions clearly**:

## Clarification Needed

### 1. [Topic]
**Question:** [The question]
**Options:**
- A) [Option A]
- B) [Option B]
**Impact:** [Why this matters for implementation]

### 2. [Topic]
...

## Assumptions Confirmed
List any assumptions in the spec that seem safe and don't need clarification.

## Ready to Proceed
Indicate which parts of the plan are clear and can proceed without changes.

---

**Important:**
- Do NOT make changes to files
- Do NOT redo research the planner already did
- Only ask questions that genuinely affect implementation decisions
- If the spec is clear enough, say "The specification is clear. Proceed to execution."
- Keep questions focused on removing blockers for the worker agent
